## Arithmetic & Number Systems in Elementary Mathematics

### Numerals, bases, and place value 数字与位值

| Symbol | English term                          | Terme français               | 中文术语              | Brief explanation                                       | Example                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| ------ | ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| —      | numeral                               | numéral                      | 数码/数字记号           | Written form representing a number (e.g., “12”, “XII”). | Example: 12<br>EN: “the numeral twelve”<br>FR: “le numéral douze” (often: “le nombre douze”)<br>中: “数字记号‘十二’”                                                                                                           |
